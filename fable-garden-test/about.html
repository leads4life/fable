About Fable Garden

Fable Garden is a small, browser-first home for enduring public-domain story traditions and fresh educational context. This structural test contains one complete fable so its reading, search, and saving patterns can be evaluated clearly.

Built to grow carefully

A future edition can add fables with distinct pages, metadata, and original commentary. Each retelling should be newly written from a traditional plot rather than copied from a modern translation.

Private by design

There are no accounts, tracking scripts, backend, or server-side database. Favorites, reading history, and themes remain on the visitor’s device when browser storage is available.

Timeless stories. Enduring lessons. The traditional fable is in the public domain. The retelling, commentary, organization, design, and original illustrations on this website were created for Fable Garden.
