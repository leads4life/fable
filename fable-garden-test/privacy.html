Privacy

Fable Garden has no user accounts, server-side database, analytics by default, advertising trackers, or sale of personal information.

Browser-local settings

Favorites, recently viewed fables, and theme preference are stored only in your browser when LocalStorage is available. Clearing browser storage removes those settings. If storage is blocked, the reading experience still works without saved preferences.

Email

Email links open your device’s email application; Fable Garden does not transmit a contact form.
